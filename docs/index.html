Designed around how families actually school

Not a bell schedule—our Week Preview and Day Detail make it obvious what’s planned, what’s done, and what’s next.

Plan the way you teach

Books, time blocks, or custom activities. Add Saxon, schedule free reading 30 minutes, or log that grocery-store math.

Flexible pacing: Skip to push the timeline, Catch-up to keep the deadline, or Do Extra to pull work forward.
Student agency: learners can choose order, skip, or double—with parent guardrails.

Documentation that makes sense

Portfolio evidence: photos/files/notes on any completion; export weekly summaries (CSV/PDF).
Progress at a glance: bars & pace indicators keep everyone aligned.
Optional calendar feed: show plans on calendars you already use.



Everything you need to start

⌛Fast planning

🔄Real-world rescheduling

Evidence gallery and exportable PDFs/CSVs.
